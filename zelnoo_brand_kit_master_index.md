# Zelnoo Brand Identity Kit — Master Index

**Version:** 1.0 Working Draft  
**Brand:** Zelnoo  
**Tagline:** Diagnostics, democratized.  
**Primary Brand Colour:** `#F08E04`

This is the master navigation document for the Zelnoo Brand Identity Kit. The full kit is split into smaller departmental documents to avoid canvas rendering issues and to make maintenance easier.

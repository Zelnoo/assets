## 1. Marketing and Sales Guidelines

### 1.1 Marketing Visual Style

Marketing creatives should look premium, modern, and healthcare-relevant.

Core style:

- White/black/orange palette.
- Strong headline in Syne.
- Simple body copy in DM Sans.
- One main visual idea per creative.
- Minimal icons and line-art.
- No cluttered discount banners.
- Use health-tech trust cues: reports, sample collection, verified labs, time, family, convenience.

### 1.2 Social Media Templates

| Platform | Recommended Size | Usage |
|---|---:|---|
| Instagram Square | 1080 × 1080 | Awareness posts, feature cards, trust messages. |
| Instagram Story / Reel Cover | 1080 × 1920 | Offers, reminders, service explainer. |
| LinkedIn Post | 1200 × 1200 | Company updates, hiring, partnerships, thought leadership. |
| LinkedIn Banner | 1584 × 396 | Company page and employee profiles. |
| WhatsApp Creative | 1080 × 1080 or 1200 × 628 | Founder sharing, local campaigns, sales follow-up. |
| Presentation Slide | 16:9 | Pitch decks, sales decks, internal reviews. |

### 1.3 Social Template Types

| Template | Layout |
|---|---|
| Feature Announcement | Black header, orange headline, white body card. |
| Educational Post | White background, orange icon, simple bullet list. |
| Trust Post | Lab/phlebotomist/report image, black overlay, orange CTA. |
| Offer Post | Orange card with black text, small white terms section. |
| Hiring Post | White background, black title, orange role tag. |
| Founder Update | Black background, orange quote highlight, logo footer. |

### 1.4 Marketing Copy Rules

Do:

- Say “book diagnostic tests” instead of vague “health solution.”
- Say “compare pricing and report timelines” instead of “cheapest tests.”
- Mention “verified partners” only where verification is operationally complete.
- Keep medical claims neutral.
- Use clear CTAs.

Avoid:

- “Guaranteed diagnosis.”
- “Best lab in the city” unless supported by evidence.
- “Instant reports” unless true.
- “100% accurate reports” because report accuracy depends on lab processes and clinical variables.
- Fear-based messaging such as “You may be sick and not know it.”

## 3. Brand Voice and Tone

### 3.1 Voice Principles

Zelnoo speaks with clarity, responsibility, and service confidence.

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Clear** | Make healthcare workflows easy to understand. |
| **Responsible** | Avoid unsupported medical or operational claims. |
| **Calm** | Reduce anxiety around tests and reports. |
| **Transparent** | Explain pricing, timing, and process openly. |
| **Helpful** | Guide the user to the next best step. |

### 3.2 Tone by Audience

| Audience | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Customers | Simple, reassuring, helpful | “Choose a convenient pickup slot and we’ll guide you through the rest.” |
| Doctors | Respectful, precise, clinical | “Track patient report status with consent-based access.” |
| Labs | Professional, operational | “Update pricing, TAT, and availability from your lab portal.” |
| Phlebotomists | Direct, task-oriented | “Confirm collection only after sample labeling is complete.” |
| Admin/Ops | Structured, exception-focused | “This order requires manual review due to delayed handover.” |
| Investors | Strategic, platform-oriented | “Zelnoo brings discovery, workflow, and quality intelligence into one diagnostics layer.” |
| Vendors | Clear, formal, transactional | “Please share the revised quotation with GST breakup and delivery timeline.” |
| Employees | Professional, respectful | “Please complete document submission before onboarding approval.” |

### 3.3 Writing Rules

Do:

- Use short sentences.
- Use active voice.
- Put the next action clearly.
- Show price, date, time, location, and status wherever relevant.
- Use “diagnostic center” or “lab/center” consistently.

Avoid:

- Medical advice unless reviewed by qualified professionals.
- Fear-based copy.
- Overpromising speed or accuracy.
- Slang in customer or partner communication.
- Excessive exclamation marks.

Include:

- Required documents checklist.
- Portal workflow overview.

Use operational and business language.

Good:

```text
Zelnoo helps diagnostic partners receive digital bookings, manage catalog visibility, update pricing, and upload reports through a structured workflow.
```

```text
Join us and become the cheapest lab in your area.
```

## 10. Photography and Imagery

### 10.1 Photography Direction

Zelnoo photography should show clean, real, trustworthy healthcare service moments.

Use:

- Clean diagnostic center environments.
- Phlebotomist preparing collection kit.
- Professional home sample collection setup.
- Secure packaging and handover.
- Lab technicians in clean workflow.
- Customers using mobile booking in calm settings.
- Doctors reviewing reports with consent.
- Report vault / health record metaphors.

Avoid:

- Fear-based patient imagery.
- Blood-heavy or graphic visuals.
- Messy lab environments.
- Overly staged stock photos with unrealistic smiles.
- Unhygienic collection scenes.
- Images suggesting Zelnoo itself performs lab testing unless operationally true.

### 10.2 Image Treatment

| Treatment | Rule |
|---|---|
| Brightness | Clean and bright, not hospital-cold. |
| Contrast | Moderate; avoid harsh shadows. |
| Colour | Warm neutrals with subtle orange accents. |
| Overlay | Use black overlay for text readability. |
| Crop | Focus on hands, kit, phone, report, or human reassurance. |
| Privacy | Never show identifiable patient information. |

### 10.3 Illustration Style

Use simple line illustrations for:

- Test tubes.
- Blood drop.
- Home pickup.
- Location pin.
- Reports.
- Calendar.
- Doctor prescription.
- Lab building.
- Shield/trust.

Illustrations should be:

- 1.5–2 px stroke.
- Rounded line caps.
- Minimal fills.
- Orange, black, grey, or white only unless semantic.

## 11. Icon System

### 11.1 Icon Style

| Property | Rule |
|---|---|
| Stroke | 1.75–2 px. |
| Shape | Rounded caps and joins. |
| Fill | Mostly outline; filled icons only for selected states. |
| Size | 16, 20, 24, 32 px. |
| Colour | Black default, orange active, grey inactive. |

### 11.2 Core Icons Required

| Category | Icons |
|---|---|
| Diagnostics | Test tube, blood drop, vial, report, microscope, scan. |
| Discovery | Search, filter, location pin, map, star, compare. |
| Booking | Calendar, clock, home, payment, receipt. |
| Operations | Route, bag, QR scan, package, handover, checklist. |
| Trust | Shield, check, verified badge, lock, consent. |
| Admin | Dashboard, table, settings, alert, dispute, refund. |
| Users | Customer, doctor, phlebotomist, lab, admin, family. |

### 11.3 Icon Usage Rules

Do:

- Use icons to support labels, not replace important text.
- Keep icon sizes consistent within a screen.
- Use orange only for active or highlighted states.
- Use a single icon family across product surfaces.

Avoid:

- Mixing filled, 3D, cartoon, and line icons.
- Using medical icons that imply emergency care or hospital treatment if not applicable.
- Using icons without accessible labels in product UI.

## 13. Compliance-Safe Brand Language

### 13.1 Healthcare Claim Rules

Use language like:

- “Book diagnostic tests.”
- “Compare available diagnostic centers.”
- “Report timelines may vary by lab and test.”
- “Home sample collection availability depends on location and slot.”
- “Reports are provided by the selected diagnostic partner.”
- “Consult a qualified doctor for medical interpretation.”

Avoid:

- “We diagnose you.”
- “Guaranteed accurate results.”
- “Instant cure / instant diagnosis.”
- “Best lab guaranteed.”
- “100% safe and error-free.”
- “Doctor-approved for everyone.”

### 13.2 Recommended Disclaimer Blocks

#### Consumer Booking

```text
Zelnoo enables diagnostic discovery, booking, logistics coordination, and report access. Test processing and reports are provided by the selected diagnostic partner. Please consult a qualified medical practitioner for interpretation of results.
```

#### Offers

```text
Offer availability may vary by location, diagnostic partner, test, and selected slot. Terms apply.
```

#### Report Access

```text
Reports are uploaded as received from the diagnostic partner. Zelnoo does not replace professional medical consultation.
```

#### Home Collection

```text
Home sample collection is subject to slot availability, service area, and test requirements.
```
